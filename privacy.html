Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Lelefon Gaming Store collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information in accordance with the Malaysia Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2010.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, email, phone number
  • Game identifiers (UIDs, Player ID)
  • Order history and payment proof
  • Device data, IP address, browser type
  • Analytics: page views, navigation patterns

3. How We Use Information

  • Process top-up orders and verify delivery
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized activities
  • Improve our service quality and customer experience
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Data Security

We implement encryption, secure storage, and strict access limitations to protect user data. Payment information is handled only by trusted third-party processors.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We only share data with:

  • Payment partners (e-wallets, banks, gateway providers)
  • Game Publishers (only when required for top-up verification)
  • Government agencies when required by law

6. Cookies

Cookies enhance your experience by enabling faster loading and analytics. You may disable cookies in browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain data as long as required for accounting, customer support, legal compliance, or fraud detection.

8. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
