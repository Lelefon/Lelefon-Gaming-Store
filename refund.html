Refund Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

Digital goods delivered through Lelefon Gaming Store are non-physical and often fulfilled instantly. As such, refund eligibility is limited and strictly regulated to protect both consumers and the integrity of our services.

2. Non-Refundable Items

You acknowledge and agree that the following items are non-refundable once delivered:

  • In-game currency (Diamonds, VP, Genesis Crystals, etc.)
  • Digital codes, vouchers, or redemption keys
  • Top-up products successfully credited to your game account

3. Eligible Refund Conditions

You may qualify for a refund only under the following circumstances:

  • Payment completed but product not delivered
  • Incorrect product delivered due to Lelefon system error
  • Duplicate payment or accidental repeated order

4. Refund Submission Requirements

You must submit the following to request a refund:

  • Order ID and payment receipt
  • Game UID and relevant screenshots
  • Accurate contact information

5. Evaluation & Processing Time

Refunds are subject to manual verification. Processing time typically ranges from 5–7 business days depending on payment channel and approval results.

6. Fraud Prevention & Abuse Policy

Lelefon reserves the right to reject refund requests involving:

  • False claims
  • Chargeback abuse
  • Misrepresentation of order details
  • Use of unauthorized or stolen payment methods
