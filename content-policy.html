Content Policy & Prohibited Content Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Purpose of This Policy

This Content Policy outlines the types of content that are permitted or prohibited on the Lelefon Gaming Store platform. It also describes the onboarding, review, and approval process for adding new content or partners. This policy applies to all digital products, partners, merchants, and publishers using the platform.

---

2. Allowed Content

The following categories of content are allowed, subject to review and approval:

2.1 Gaming-Related Digital Content

  • In-game credits and currencies (e.g. Valorant Points, Diamonds, UC, Genesis Crystals)
  • Game vouchers, prepaid cards, and digital codes
  • Direct top-up services using valid game IDs (UID-based)
  • Official digital gift cards for supported games

2.2 Marketing & Informational Content

  • Accurate game descriptions and pricing information
  • Promotional banners that are truthful and non-misleading
  • Game-related instructions and usage guides
---

3. Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited and will not be listed or distributed on the platform under any circumstances:

3.1 Illegal or High-Risk Activities

  • Money laundering or terrorism financing activities
  • Sale of stolen, hacked, or illegally obtained digital assets
  • Cheat software, bots, exploits, or hacking tools
  • Any content that violates Malaysian law or international regulations

3.2 Adult, Sexual, or Harmful Content

  • Pornographic or sexually explicit material
  • Content involving minors in any sexual or exploitative manner
  • Content promoting violence, self-harm, or abuse

3.3 Gambling & Chance-Based Content

  • Online gambling, betting, or casino-related services
  • Lottery or chance-based prize mechanisms involving real money

3.4 Fraudulent or Misleading Content

  • False advertising or misleading product claims
  • Impersonation of official publishers or brands
  • Unauthorized use of trademarks or copyrighted assets
---

4. Partner & Content Onboarding Policy

Lelefon Gaming Store maintains a structured onboarding process to ensure that all content and partners comply with platform standards and regulatory requirements.

4.1 Pre-Onboarding Review

  • Verification of business identity and contact details
  • Review of partner legitimacy and operational background
  • Confirmation of rights or authorization to distribute digital products
  • Initial screening against prohibited content categories

4.2 Content Review & Approval Process

  • Manual review of product descriptions, images, and pricing
  • Compliance checks against platform and publisher policies
  • Assessment of potential legal, regulatory, or reputational risks

Only content that successfully passes all review stages will be approved and published on the platform.

4.3 Rejection & Removal

Lelefon reserves the right to reject, suspend, or remove any content or partner that:

  • Violates this Content Policy
  • Provides false or misleading information
  • Fails to meet compliance or onboarding requirements
---

5. Ongoing Monitoring & Enforcement

Approved content is subject to ongoing monitoring. Lelefon may conduct audits, investigate complaints, and take immediate action if policy violations are identified. Enforcement actions may include warnings, delisting, account suspension, or termination.
